biomimetic-prompts: A Structured Corpus of Biomimetic Prompt Anchors for LLMs
Authors/Creators

    COYAUD, Denis (Researcher)

Description

A structured corpus of biomimetic prompt anchors for large language models (LLMs),
  mapping biological organisms to specific behavioral techniques.
  Includes a validated Core Marine (11 prompts targeting model behavioral biases
  such as verbosity, sycophancy, hallucination, equivocation, cultural universalism,
  and anachronism), an Extended Biome (38 prompts covering classic prompt engineering
  techniques via biological metaphors), and a meta-orchestrator (the Queen) that
  autonomously selects and activates the right anchor per session.
  Prompts are written in French. Documentation is in English.
